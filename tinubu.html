About Us

Coalition of middle-belt youth support is a political movement established in 2022 in order to accomplish the presidency of Tinubu and Shettima.

Mission

Coalition of middle-belt youth support is a political movement established in 2022 in order to accomplish the presidency of Tinubu and Shettima

Vision

Coalition of middle-belt youth support is a political movement established in 2022 in order to accomplish the presidency of Tinubu and Shettima.

Objective

This great movement have the following objectives or focal point which are:
1) To sensitize the Middle Belt Youths on the need for full participation in 2023 General Elections and total support for Tinubu and Shettima.
2) To mobilize Youths to obtain their PVC.
3) To team up with other youth groups and organization for total support for Tinubu and Shettima.
4) To install a deliverer of Middle Belt votes to Tinubu and Shettima as Nigeria President and Vice President in 2023 General Election.
5) To encourage the Youths to come out in mass in all election days, cast their votes and protect their votes without violence across the Middle Belt.
6) To canvass for 10 million votes across the Middle Belt States for Tinubu and Shettima. 7) To ensure 100 percent winning in all the Middle Belt States.
8) To take over the social media space for sensitization of the teaming youths.
9) To mobilize the grassroots and establish community support group for Tinubu and Shettima.
This great movement choose to support Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu because:
He is a unique politician among politicians in Nigerian politics who believe in one Nigeria
