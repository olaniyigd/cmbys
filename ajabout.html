About

Coalition of Middle Belt Youth Support for Tinubu and Shettima is a political movement established to ensure that the APC Presidential candidate emerge winner in the General election. The movemnet covered the eight states in the region which are Kaduna, Kogi, Kwara, Plateau, Niger, Nassarawa, Benue and F.C.T Abuja.

Mission

Our Mission Is To Ensure 100 Percent Winning For The APC Aspirant In All The Middle Belt States And Project The Values, Ideas, Leadership Skills And Personality Of Bola Ahmed Tinubu As The Most Efficient Leader Nigerians Need To Drive The Country From A Third To A First World Nation.

Vision

The Coalition Of Middle Belt Youth's Support For Tinubu Shettima 2023 (COMBYS4TS) Will Strive To Sensitize The Teeming Middle Belt Youth's On The Need For Full Participation In 2023 General Elections And Total Support For Tinubu Shettima And All APC Aspirant Across All The Nine (9) Middle Belt States Towards Ensuring The Emergence Of A True Democrat, His Excellency Sen. Bola Ahmed Come 2023.

Aim and Objectives

Our Aims And Objectives Is To Mobilize The Grassroot And Establish Community Support Group By Encouraging The Youth's To Come Out In Mass In All Election Days, Cast Their Votes And Protect Their Votes Without Violence Across The Middle Belt States And To Also Take Over The Social Media Space For Sensitization Of The Teeming Middle Belt Youth's Towards Ensuring They Obtain Their PVC And Vote For All APC Aspirant In The 2023 General Elections.
